Siyuan Yan

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Monash Medical AI Group (MMAI) at Monash University, working with A/Prof. Zongyuan Ge, Prof. Peter Soyer, and Prof. Monika Janda.

I am primally interested in trustworthy AI in computer vision and medical image analysis, i.e. out-of-domain generalization, debasing, and explainability. Before coming to Monash University, I received my master's degree at the Australian National University under the supervision of Prof. Nick Barnes and Dr. Jing Zhang.

    Research

    Most of my recent research is about understanding deep learning generalization behavior under distribution shift. I am also interested in probabilistic generative model, data-centric learning, weakly supervised learning, and AI for dermatology.
